How to use {{ ingredient.name }} in smoothies

A simple way to use {{ ingredient.name }} is to start small, blend, taste, and adjust. If you're not sure what pairs well, open the analyzer and experiment with 2–4 ingredients.

  • Start with a small amount first, then increase gradually based on taste and tolerance.
