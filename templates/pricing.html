Frequently Asked Questions

Is PureFyul really free during Beta?

Yes! PureFyul is completely free during our Beta period. You get full access to all current features including ingredient analysis, smoothie building, allergen information (ingredient-based), and Goal-based ingredient discovery. No credit card required, no hidden fees.

How long will the Beta last?

We're committed to keeping PureFyul free during Beta as we continue to improve and add features. We'll provide advance notice before any changes to our pricing model, and Beta users will be grandfathered into special benefits.

Is my data private and secure?

We do not sell your personal information. We use only the data needed to run and secure the service (account login, preferences, and basic operational logs). We may share data with service providers (hosting, database, email) strictly to operate the app. If we enable ads, Google and its partners may use cookies for advertising and measurement. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How accurate is the nutrition data?

Our nutrition values are compiled from referenced databases and sources we list. Values can vary by brand and preparation, so treat them as estimates.
